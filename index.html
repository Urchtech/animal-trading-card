Goldfish

Goldfish have two sets of paired fins and 3 sets of single fins. They don't have barbels, sensory organs some fish have that act like taste buds.

  • Scientific Name:Carassius auratus
  • Average Size: 4.7- 16.1 inches
  • Lifespan: 10-30 years
  • Habitat: Fresh water bodies
  • weight: 0.2-0.6 pounds
  • Diet: Omnivore

The goldfish is a fresh water fish in the family of cyprinidae of order cyprinformes. It is commonly kept as a pet in indoor aquariums, and is one of the most popular aquarium fish.
